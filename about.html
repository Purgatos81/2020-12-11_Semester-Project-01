ABOUT US

Come join us at the Community Science Museum where we´re committed to making science accessible to all.

OUR GOAL

Over the course of human history, science has developed from Our early understanding of fire, Wind, water, and Earth to Explore everything from galaxies far away to the very building Blocks of life itself.

The aim of Our museum is tocreate a Space where everyone can experience the Wonders of Our discoveries and perhaps even ignite a lifelong passion to continiue the Exploration of the world around us.

We belive science should not be confined to the textbook, but brought to live through exhibits. This is why we have over 1000 different exhibits on the many varied subjects of science to Explore. Many of these exhibits are designed for you to interact With and play around to see science come to life (apart from the dinosaur exhibitts - they only come to life at night when everyone´s gone home).
